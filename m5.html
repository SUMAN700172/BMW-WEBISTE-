THE M5 SEDAN

The BMW M5 Touring elevates the fusion of standout driving dynamics with superior everyday usability and
long-distance capability for which BMW M models are renowned to a whole new level. The combination of a high-revving V8
engine with M TwinPower Turbo technology and an electric motor results in a new, impressive experience of typical M power -
to deliver intoxicating performance on road and track alike. Prominently flared wheel arches and side skirts as well as the M
specific design of the front and rear aprons result in an extremely powerful appearance, that makes the third generation of
the M5 Touring the ultimate high-performance all-rounder.
